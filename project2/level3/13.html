Double, double toil and trouble; Fire burn and caldron bubble. Fillet of a fenny snake, In the caldron boil and bake;

Eye

of newt and toe of frog, Wool of bat and tongue of dog,
Adder's fork and blind-worm's sting,

Lizard's leg and howlet's wing,

 For a charm of powerful trouble,
Like a hell-broth boil and bubble.

Double, double toil and trouble;

 Fire burn and caldron bubble. Cool it with a baboon's blood,
Then the charm is firm and good.
